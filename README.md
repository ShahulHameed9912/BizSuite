# BizSuite
This app is a modern business management solution designed to help entrepreneurs and small businesses monitor performance, track orders, analyze revenue, and manage daily operations from one place.
ðŸ”¹ Dashboard
    The dashboard provides a quick overview of business activity with smart summary cards displaying:
      * Total Products
      * Total Orders
      * Total Clients
      * Revenue progress
Each card includes visual progress indicators, making it easy to understand performance at a glance. A revenue chart with daily, weekly, and monthly filters helps users track trends and compare growth over time.

ðŸ”¹ Analytics
The analytics section offers deeper insights into business performance, including:
Total sales overview
Average sales statistics
Interactive area charts for order analysis
Trending items with sales increase or decrease indicators
This helps users identify top-performing products and understand customer demand patterns.
ðŸ”¹ Orders
The orders module allows users to:
View order statistics through bar charts
Filter data by monthly, weekly, or today views
Access a detailed order list showing customer names, dates, order amounts, and order status
This section is designed for fast tracking and efficient order management.
ðŸ”¹ Settings
The settings screen provides customization and control options such as:
Language selection
Currency preferences
Date format configuration
Notification management for orders, payments, and weekly reports
A referral section encourages user growth with promotional offers.
ðŸ”¹ Profile
The profile page displays complete user and business information including:
Profile details (name, email, phone)
Business name
Key metrics such as total orders, revenue, and clients
Users can easily update personal or business information from this section.
